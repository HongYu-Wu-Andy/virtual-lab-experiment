# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, evidence-oriented community. Be constructive, welcome different levels of experience, distinguish evidence from opinion, and critique ideas without attacking people.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disclosure of private information, publication of credentials or confidential datasets, and repeated disruption of technical or scientific discussion.

## Scientific responsibility

Do not present model predictions as experimental validation. Report limitations, uncertainty, safety concerns, conflicts of interest, and material errors promptly. Never pressure contributors to disclose restricted data or credentials.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Reports will be reviewed confidentially and may result in warnings, removal of content, temporary restrictions, or permanent exclusion from project spaces.

This policy applies to the repository, discussions, issues, pull requests, and project-related public interactions.
